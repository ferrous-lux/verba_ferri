There Is No Secret Word

The game does not have an answer. You supply the answer. The dictionary simply agrees with you as emphatically as it can. You have never been wrong. You will never be wrong.

130-Dimensional Vector Matching

Every word is encoded as a one-hot vector in a 130-dimensional space. The dot product of your guess against 1682 dictionary vectors determines the optimal arrangement of green, yellow, and grey tiles. This is real mathematics, deployed exclusively to make you feel smart.

Compiled to WebAssembly

Written in Rust. Burned to WASM. The entire game logic executes in your browser at near-native speed, which is approximately 4000× faster than you will ever need for a 5-letter word game. We did it because we could.

Looks Exactly Like Wordle

Green tiles. Yellow tiles. Grey tiles. A 6×5 grid. It looks, sounds, and feels like Wordle — except the game secretly has nothing to hide because there is nothing to guess. The tension is purely decorative.

Zero Ads. Zero Tracking. Zero Point.

There are no advertisements. There is no analytics. There is no high score. There is no leaderboard. The Share button exports an SVG of your grid so you can post it somewhere and nobody will know the difference. Your secret is safe.
